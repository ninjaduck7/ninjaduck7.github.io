物理随笔1 -- 从求解基态角度出发联系统计力学和优化问题 // Physics Gossip 1 – Relating Statistical Mechanics and Optimization Problems from the Perspective of Solving the Ground State

Felix
2025-02-12 (Updated: 2025-02-12)
Physics
gossip, physics

我们生活中的许多问题实际上可以映射到一个解空间中。在这个空间里，问题的每一种配置（config）都有一个对应的能量值。求解问题的过程，就像是在波涛汹涌的大海上驾驶一艘小船，目标是找到那个能量值的全局最小值，也就是海面上最低的地方。

Many problems in our lives can be mapped onto a solution space. In this space, each configuration (config) of the problem corresponds to a specific energy value. Solving the problem is akin to navigating a small boat across a choppy sea, where the goal is to find the global minimum of the energy, the lowest point on the water’s surface.

能量景观描绘了一个物理系统所有可能的微观状态（或称配置）以及每个状态对应的能量值。在这个景观中，系统的状态空间由不同的配置组成，每一个配置都有一个特定的能量值与之相联系。可以把它想象成一幅起伏的山脉图，山脊代表着能量较高的状态，而山谷则是能量较低的区域。系统的演化过程，实际上就是在这座山脉中寻找最低谷的过程——即寻找最低的能量状态。

The energy landscape describes all possible microstates (or configurations) of a physical system and their corresponding energy values. In this landscape, the system’s state space consists of various configurations, each associated with a specific energy value. One can imagine this as a mountainous terrain, where the peaks represent high-energy states, and the valleys correspond to regions of lower energy. The system’s evolution is essentially the process of navigating this landscape, seeking the lowest valley—the state of minimal energy.
